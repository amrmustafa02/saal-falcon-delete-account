Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained for a limited period if required for legal, regulatory, security, fraud prevention, or financial record-keeping purposes.

  • Transaction or payment-related records

Retention Period

Retained data, if any, will only be kept for as long as necessary to meet legal, regulatory, or legitimate business obligations, after which it will be securely deleted or anonymized.
